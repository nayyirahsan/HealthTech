# AI Career Copilot for Healthcare Professionals

## Summary

AI Career Copilot is a career intelligence platform designed to help healthcare professionals — starting with medical residents — make informed career decisions. While healthcare careers follow highly structured training paths, there is currently no structured system that provides transparent guidance on competitiveness, career progression, or salary benchmarks.

Most residents rely on informal sources such as word of mouth, online forums, or inconsistent mentorship to determine how competitive they are for fellowships or what they should prioritize during training. This creates uncertainty in a profession where strategic career decisions have long-term consequences.

The platform provides a personalized dashboard that helps users understand their current standing, define career goals, and identify the steps required to reach them. By analyzing a user’s profile — including specialty, training year, research experience, leadership roles, and certifications — the system generates a structured career roadmap and highlights skill gaps that may affect competitiveness for fellowships or advanced positions.

The initial MVP focuses on several core capabilities: a profile builder for structured career data, a career roadmap generator that outlines milestones for specific goals (such as cardiology fellowship), a skill gap analysis dashboard, a salary benchmarking tool based on location and specialty, and an AI-powered career advisor that answers questions about competitiveness and career planning.

The product is strategically positioned because it can be sold directly to individuals without requiring hospital integration or access to patient data. This avoids regulatory complexity and enterprise sales friction while providing clear value to users.

The initial business model is a subscription priced around $10–20 per month for access to premium insights, personalized roadmaps, and AI guidance. Over time, the platform could expand to include residency program analytics dashboards, mentorship marketplaces, and physician contract analysis tools.

The long-term vision is to build the central career analytics platform for healthcare professionals, helping clinicians navigate their entire career lifecycle with better data and guidance.
